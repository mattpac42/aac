# CORE ENHANCEMENT PRINCIPLES

## Scarcity Psychology
- Three scarcity types: Limited Supply (only X available), Limited Bonuses (bonuses disappear), Never Available Again (one-time opportunity)
- Honest scarcity based on actual capacity creates authentic urgency
- Truth-based capacity messaging: "I can only handle 10 clients per month" (if true)
- Scarcity stretches value-to-price discrepancy in prospect's mind

## Urgency Tactics
- Four urgency types: Cohort-based (start dates), Seasonal (time-based), Pricing/Bonus (deadline-driven), Exploding Opportunity (market timing)
- Urgency forces decision-making and overcomes procrastination
- Combine with scarcity for maximum psychological impact
- Must be authentic and aligned with business model

## Bonus Strategy
- Single offer < Components stacked as bonuses (psychological principle)
- 11 Bonus Bullets strategy: special names, benefits in title, storytelling, proof, vivid mental pictures, price tags
- Trim from core offer and reframe as valuable bonuses
- Each bonus needs individual value articulation and pricing
- Arnold Fundraiser example: $5.4M from 100 people using bonus stacking

## Guarantee Psychology
- Four guarantee types: Unconditional (full refund, no questions), Conditional (refund with requirements), Anti (pay more if it doesn't work), Implied (reputation-based)
- Stronger guarantees increase conversions 2-4x
- Service guarantees (author's favorite): "I'll keep working until you get the result"
- 10 conditional guarantee types available for different business models
- Guarantee economics: conversion lift must exceed refund cost

# ENHANCEMENT IMPLEMENTATION FRAMEWORK

## Scarcity Strategy Development

**Three Scarcity Types**:
1. **Limited Supply**: "Only 10 spots available this month"
2. **Limited Bonuses**: "First 20 buyers get XYZ bonus"
3. **Never Available Again**: "This offer won't be repeated"

**Honest Scarcity Checklist**:
- [ ] Based on actual capacity constraint
- [ ] Truthful and verifiable
- [ ] Aligned with business model
- [ ] Creates authentic urgency

**Deliverable**: Scarcity Strategy Document

## Urgency Tactics Design

**Four Urgency Types**:
1. **Cohort-based**: Enrollment deadlines, program start dates
2. **Seasonal**: Holiday offers, annual events, quarter-end
3. **Pricing/Bonus**: Early bird pricing, deadline-driven bonuses
4. **Exploding Opportunity**: Market timing, regulatory changes

**Urgency Implementation**:
- Clear deadline communication
- Countdown timers and reminders
- Consequence messaging (what they lose by waiting)

**Deliverable**: Urgency Strategy Document

## Bonus Stacking Architecture

**11 Bonus Bullets Framework**:
1. Give it a special name
2. Tell what it is
3. Tell what benefit it provides (title should convey transformation)
4. Tell them how (explain mechanism)
5. Provide proof it works
6. Paint vivid mental picture of achievement
7. Provide price tag or value
8. Stack multiple bonuses
9. Make bonuses feel "trimmed from core" and repackaged
10. Each bonus should solve specific problem
11. Total bonus value should exceed core offer price

**Deliverable**: Bonus Architecture Plan with copy templates

## Guarantee Strategy Selection

**Guarantee Type Decision Matrix**:

| Type | Best For | Risk Level | Conversion Impact |
|------|----------|------------|-------------------|
| Unconditional | High-trust brands | High | 2-4x lift |
| Conditional | Service businesses | Medium | 1.5-3x lift |
| Service-based | Result-oriented | Low | 2-3x lift |
| Anti-guarantee | Confidence play | Very Low | 1-2x lift |
| Performance | Outcome-driven | Variable | 2-5x lift |

**10 Conditional Guarantee Types**:
1. Outsized Refund (return more than paid)
2. Service (keep working until result achieved)
3. Modified Service (partial refund + service)
4. Credit-Based (store credit vs cash)
5. Personal Service (done-for-you guarantee)
6. Hotel + Airfare (cover travel costs)
7. Wage-Payment (pay their hourly wage)
8. Release of Service (stop payment if unsatisfied)
9. Delayed Second Payment (pay rest only if satisfied)
10. First Outcome (refund until first result achieved)

**Deliverable**: Guarantee Strategy Analysis with copy
