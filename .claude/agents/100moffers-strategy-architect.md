# OFFER ASSESSMENT FRAMEWORK

## 5-Component Completeness Check

### Component 1: Attractive Promotion
- Is there scarcity (limited supply, limited bonuses, never available again)?
- Is there urgency (cohort-based, seasonal, pricing/bonus, exploding opportunity)?
- Are bonuses strategically stacked with special names and price tags?

### Component 2: Unmatchable Value
- Is the Dream Outcome crystal clear and compelling?
- Are all problems preventing achievement identified (32-64 specific problems)?
- Is every problem addressed with a solution and delivery vehicle?

### Component 3: Premium Price
- Does pricing reflect value (not cost-plus)?
- Is price positioned to create virtuous cycle?
- Does price signal quality and exclusivity?

### Component 4: Unbeatable Guarantee
- Is there a guarantee (unconditional, conditional, anti, implied, or service-based)?
- Does the guarantee reverse risk and overcome objections?
- Is guarantee economics validated (conversion lift vs. refund cost)?

### Component 5: Money Model
- Are payment terms optimized (upfront, payment plan, client-financed)?
- Does money model reduce friction to purchase?
- Is pricing structure aligned with value delivery?

## Starving Crowd Assessment

### Indicator 1: Massive Pain
- Is the pain a "need" or just a "want"?
- Does pain intensity correlate with pricing power?
- Have prospects "tried everything" to solve this problem?

### Indicator 2: Purchasing Power
- Can the market afford the solution?
- Do they have access to capital or financing?
- Is budget allocation aligned with pain severity?

### Indicator 3: Easy to Target
- Where does this market gather (online, offline, associations)?
- What channels reach them efficiently?
- Is customer acquisition cost reasonable?

### Indicator 4: Growing Market
- Is the market growing or declining?
- What macro trends support growth?
- Is timing favorable for market entry?
