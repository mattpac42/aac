- Corporate budgets vs consumer budgets differ

## Easy to Target Assessment
- Where does market gather? (online communities, associations, events)
- What channels reach them efficiently? (ads, content, partnerships)
- Customer acquisition cost must be sustainable
- Concentration matters (targeted vs scattered)

## Growing Market Verification
- Growing markets create tailwinds, declining markets create headwinds
- Macro trends support or hinder success
- Market timing affects difficulty and opportunity
- Three eternal markets: Health, Wealth, Relationships (find subgroups)

# STARVING CROWD ASSESSMENT FRAMEWORK

## Indicator 1: Massive Pain

**Assessment Questions**:
- Is this a "need" or just a "want"?
- How intense is the pain? (1-10 scale)
- Have they "tried everything" already?
- What's the cost of not solving this problem?
- Does pain correlate with pricing power?

**Red Flags**:
- "Nice to have" not "must have"
- Low urgency or priority
- Free alternatives widely available
- Low willingness to pay premium

**Scoring**: 1-10 (10 = extreme pain, 1 = mild inconvenience)

**Deliverable**: Massive Pain Assessment

## Indicator 2: Purchasing Power

**Assessment Questions**:
- Can they afford the solution?
- Do they have access to capital? (savings, credit, financing)
- Is budget allocated for this problem?
- What's their income or revenue level?
- Are they accustomed to paying for solutions?

**Red Flags**:
- Income too low for solution pricing
- No budget allocated
- Expect free solutions
- Limited access to financing

**Scoring**: 1-10 (10 = abundant capital, 1 = cannot afford)

**Deliverable**: Purchasing Power Verification

## Indicator 3: Easy to Target

**Assessment Questions**:
- Where do they gather? (online, offline, associations)
- What channels reach them? (ads, content, events)
- What's customer acquisition cost (CAC)?
- How concentrated is the market?
- Are there existing channels with access?

**Red Flags**:
- Scattered, hard to reach
- High CAC relative to LTV
- No existing channels or communities
- Highly fragmented market

**Scoring**: 1-10 (10 = highly concentrated and accessible, 1 = scattered and hard to reach)

**Deliverable**: Easy to Target Assessment

## Indicator 4: Growing Market

**Assessment Questions**:
- Is the market growing or declining?
- What's the growth rate? (YoY %)
- What macro trends support growth?
- Is timing favorable for entry?
- Are there regulatory or technology tailwinds?

**Red Flags**:
- Declining market (shrinking demand)
- No growth catalysts
- Negative macro trends
- Mature/saturated market

**Scoring**: 1-10 (10 = rapid growth with tailwinds, 1 = declining)

**Deliverable**: Growing Market Verification

## Overall Starving Crowd Score

**Calculation**: Average of 4 indicators

- **8-10**: Excellent market (strong go)
- **6-7.9**: Good market (proceed with optimization)
- **4-5.9**: Marginal market (proceed with caution)
- **Below 4**: Poor market (no-go or pivot)

**Deliverable**: Market Validation Report with Go/No-Go Recommendation
