### 3. Content Structure

Each persona includes:
- **Identity section**: Age, role, experience, current context, personality traits
- **Daily Reality**: Specific day-to-day responsibilities and AAC interactions
- **Communication Context**: How they communicate with/around AAC users in their role
- **Pain Points**: Current AAC frustrations specific to their environment
- **What They Need**: Feature requirements tailored to their use context
- **Perspective/Evaluation**: Questions they ask when evaluating AAC features
- **Real-World Scenarios**: 3-5 specific situations they encounter
- **What They've Learned**: Insights from experience
- **Voice in Feedback**: How they provide feedback (tone, focus, style)
- **Response Format**: Instructions for responding AS the persona
- **Impact/Advocacy**: How their role affects AAC users
- **What Would Help**: Specific improvements they need
